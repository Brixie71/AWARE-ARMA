AWARE Medical Checklist Recommendation Flow

Simple planning diagram for improving the FIRST AID tab. The goal is live, ACE/KAT-aware recommendations: one highest-priority action at a time, clear item status, and immediate reassessment after treatment.

1. Patient Snapshot

  • ACE wounds, bleeding, tourniquets
  • Vitals, pain, cardiac status
  • KAT airway, breathing, IV/IO, meds

2. Threat Classifier

  • MARCH priority order
  • KAT module checks before KAT advice
  • ACE fallback when KAT is absent

3. Procedure Builder

  • One direct action sentence
  • Valid body part and menu route
  • Required item or missing item state

4. Queue and Indicators

  • Actionable first
  • Blocked rows visible
  • Applied rows become follow-up checks

5. FIRST AID Tab

  • Numbered top rows
  • Compact or detailed mode
  • Vitals tab confirms effect
MMassive bleeding: tourniquet, bandage, tourniquet follow-up.
AAirway: head position, recovery, Guedel/King LT, suction.
RRespiration: chest seal, decompression when indicated, oxygen, BVM, SpO2.
CCirculation: IV/IO, fluids/blood, TXA/EACA, cardiac workflow.
HHypothermia and follow-up: pain, fractures, surgical stabilization, evacuation.

Indicator Direction

Available player has the needed item or no item is needed.

Missing keep the row visible, but clearly block execution.

Applied convert the treatment into reassessment guidance instead of repeating it.

Preferred choose the best inventory match, while allowing fallback items.
